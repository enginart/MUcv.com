About

CantoVario enables users to take songs and mash them up to create something new and perhaps unexpected.

[Some context: CantoVario had its origins in an MIT doctoral thesis (“Musical Variations from a Chaotic Mapping”) and was written up in the Boston Globe and in Science. Olin College of Engineering and the MIT Venture Mentoring Service have helped guide and support this work. Patents 9286876 and 9286877]
